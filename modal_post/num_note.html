Welcome to my book...

    To the small n' fluffy alpacas,
I cordially invite you to this book.

At this moment of writing I am a computer science student. I enjoy computers and art. Animation is a perfect combination of those things. I am a self taught 3D animator, when I found Modo (a 3D application by Foundry) I was instantly amazed. The power of animation for a single user is something I thought was impposible. Like many applications it takes practice and patience to study and learn every aspect of 3D. What made me want to use Modo over other 3D applications was it's design and approach to 3D. Through my studies I have created this digital book.

Thanks for your support!
